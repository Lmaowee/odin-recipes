Ramen

Description

A delicious ramen thats easy to make at home and healthy

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 ounce of noodles
  • 1 slice of american cheese

steps

  1. gather all the Ingredients
  2. bring water to boil in a saucepan. add ramen noodle and cook until tender, about 2 minutes
  3. pour out water, then stir in seasoning packet and cheese until well blended
