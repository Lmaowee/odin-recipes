Fried Chicken

Description

An easy how to make Fried chicken

Ingredients

  • 4 pound chicken cut into pieces
  • 1 cup of buttermilk
  • 2 cups of all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika powder
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 quarts of vegetable oil

Steps

  1. take your cut up chicken
  2. put the flour in a large plastic bag
  3. dip the chicken pieces into the buttermilk
  4. place the coated chicken on a cookie sheet
  5. fill the skillet with the vegetable oil
  6. put in as many chicken as the skillet can hold
  7. when browned, lower the heat down and let it sit for 10 minutes
  8. serve
